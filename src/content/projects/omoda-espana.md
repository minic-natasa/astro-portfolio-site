## Overview

Omoda and JAECOO entered the Spanish market with a full SUV lineup and needed a web presence that felt local, not just translated. The brief wasn't to adapt copy but to build a digital experience that matched how Spanish buyers actually research and purchase cars.

My role was WordPress development and CMS setup.

## The Problem

Launching a car brand in a new market means competing with players who already own the digital real estate. A generic template wasn't going to cut it. The site needed to handle a full model lineup, connect buyers to physical dealers, support ongoing promotional campaigns and do all of it in a way that felt native to the market.

## What I Built

**Product Pages**

Dedicated pages for each model across both lineups: OMODA 5, 7 and 9, and JAECOO 5, 7 and 8. Each page covers full specs, available configurations, pricing and active promotional materials. The structure makes it easy to update models and campaigns without rebuilding anything.

**Dealer and Services Integration**

A dealer locator connects online visitors to physical showrooms across Spain. Test drive booking is built into the flow so interested buyers can go from researching a model to scheduling a test drive without leaving the site. Post-sale service pages cover owners who are already past the purchase stage.

**Campaign Support**

The CMS is set up to handle time-limited promotions and model launches without developer involvement. The marketing team can publish and update campaigns on their own.

**Responsive Experience**

Car buyers research on mobile and make decisions on desktop. Both experiences are smooth and consistent. Layout, interactions and page speed were tested across device types.

## Tech Stack

- WordPress
- HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Spanish market localization
- CMS configured for independent content management
