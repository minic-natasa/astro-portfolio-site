## Overview

Omoda España is a fully localized WordPress site built for the Spanish market. The site covers the complete Omoda model lineup with detailed product information, purchase options, and promotional campaign support — adapted for local language, market expectations, and user flows.

## What I Built

**Localized WordPress Site**

Implemented a market-specific WordPress site with full Spanish localization — content, navigation, and user-facing copy adapted for the target audience. Product pages include detailed specs, available configurations, and promotional materials for active campaigns.

**Dealer & Services Integration**

Integrated a dealer locator, test drive booking, and post-sale service pages — connecting potential buyers to physical touchpoints across Spain while keeping the digital experience smooth and consistent.

**Responsive Experience**

Ensured a seamless experience across devices, with layout and interactions tested and refined for both desktop and mobile users.

## Tech Stack

- **CMS:** WordPress
- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **Localization:** Spanish market adaptation
